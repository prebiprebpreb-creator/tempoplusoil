About Tempo Plus

Founded in 2004, Tempo Plus has grown into one of the region's most reliable names in lubricant distribution. As the official franchise partner of Orlen, we supply authentic, certified oils for passenger cars, trucks, agricultural machinery, marine engines and industrial applications.

Our operational hubs in Kosovo and Slovakia allow us to serve both the Balkans and Central Europe efficiently — while our logistics network fulfills orders throughout the entire European Union.

  • Certified Orlen products — direct from the manufacturer
  • Two decades of distribution experience
  • B2B and B2C ordering available
  • Fast shipping across all EU member states
